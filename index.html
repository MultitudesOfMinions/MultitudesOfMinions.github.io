This is an idle tower-offense game.

Your minions will spawn from the left and travel along the path until they reach a tower within their attack range.
After attacking they will have to reload before attacking again. Your main objective is to get your minions as far along the path as possible.

Buy upgrades for your minions in the 'T0 Upgrades' tab. Upgrades are small but stack multiplicatively; even if it seems like they have no effect at first just keep going!
'Regroup' in the 'T0 Upgrades' tab to reset T0 progress and gain additional resources for more powerful upgrades.

You have unlocked T1 upgrades. These will not reset when you 'Regroup'.
'Promote' in the 'T1 Upgrades' tab to reset T0 & T1 progress and gain additional resources for more powerful upgrades.

You have unlocked T2 upgrades. These will not reset when you 'Promote'.

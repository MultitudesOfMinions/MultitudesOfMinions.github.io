Display Details:

This is an idle tower-offense game. Minions spawn from the spawn pool and are then Deployed to the battle field. The further you get from your home the longer it takes to Deploy.

Your minions will enter the battle field from the left and travel along the path until they reach a tower within their attack range.
After attacking they will have to reload before attacking again.
If any minions can't keep up they are left behind; only minions that press the attack are worth keeping.

I made this game in my limited free time by hand in notepad++ and Caret with pure JS to see what it would be like making something like this without any addiitonal libraries. I don't plan on adding any monitization, but if I ever do it will never be required.
I hope you like it, but if not don't play it.

Armory tab has new equipment to increase attack and health. Upgrades are small but stack multiplicatively; even if it seems like they have no effect at first just keep going!

Regroup in the 'Armory' tab will reset the Armory and gain additional resources for more powerful upgrades.
Money Pit only increases the resources gained from Regroup, useful for dumping extra resources after upgrade limit has been reached.

Check back here after unlocking additional content if you have any questions.

You have unlocked the Gym. This will not reset when you 'Regroup'.
The Gym has training for your minions to become better, faster, stronger.
You can unlock new minions.

There are also additional options available in the options tab.

'Evolve' in the 'Gym' tab to reset the Armory & Gym to gain additional resources for more powerful upgrades.

You have unlocked the lab. These will not reset when you 'Evolve'.
Here you can genetically motify your Minions to spawn more and more frequently.

You can also unlock the elements to fight for you. Element do not stop when attacking.

'Promote' in the 'Lab' tab to reset the Armory, Gym, and Lab to gain additional resources for more powerful upgrades.

You have unlocked the Office and Bosses. These will not reset when you 'Promote'. With these bosses as your middle management you will become even more powerful.

Boss Agression determines how far forward the Boss should move.
When a Boss is engaging an enemy it cannot retreat.

The 'Office' has Boss enhancements and upgrades 'Minions Per Spawn'.
Minions Per Spawn also effects how much each minion counts towards 'Active Minions'. 2 Minions Per Spawn, each minion counts as .5; 3 Minions Per Spawn, each minion counds as ~.3.

'Ascend' in the 'Office' to reset the Armory, Gym, Lab, and Office to gain additional resources.

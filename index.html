General Game Information

Welcome to The Path! The Towers have been holding back the poor Underlings, trapped, unable to click buttons.

The small colorless Underlings are weak and are not upgradable but have a short spawn cycle and spawn continuously.
With your advanced button clicking skills you can expand beyond these basic drab Underlings to more powerful Minions and Beyond!

To prove your ability to click buttons you will initially start with a very limited number of Buttons and Minions, as you continue to prove how well you click buttons you will gain access to more buttons and more Minions.

This is an Idle/Incremental Tower-Offense game. As an Idle/Incremental game it will progress without much input and your Invaders will get incrementally stronger over time; some browsers may slow down the game if it isn't the active tab. As a Tower-Offense game your objective is to destroy as many defenders as possible.
There is currently not an end or win condition and the game objective is simply to make more powerful Invaders. Some game mechanics do have limits such as Move Speed to avoid game breaking.

Each Castle is the home of at least one Hero that needs vanquishing, when all the vanquishing is done the Castle collapses. Towers and Heroes get stronger as you progress

Boss

Bosses are very powerful allies with unique abilities. Only one boss can be active at a time. With your new Boss leadership you also have some additional control over the minions. There is an Advanced Tactics options in the Minions tab; this will keep minions at the begining of the path until they have reached capacity or a Boss is summoned.

Select Boss To Summon determines which boss will be summoned next.
Boss Position determines how far the Boss should move.
Active Ability button will activate your current bosses active.

Inventory/Items

This is also where you manage your Inventory. Equipped item effects are active at all times even if there is no current Boss.
The checkboxes next to the item slots filter which items are displayed.
The button with the lock icon (ðŸ”“/ðŸ”’) can lock/unlock an item. Items that are Locked and/or Equipped cannot be sold.

Minions

Minions spawn from the Spawn Pool into the Baracks and are then Deployed to active duty.
You can view your current Minions, Spawn Pool, and Baracks in the Minions tab by clicking the Minions button in the menu on the left.
You have a limited number of Active Minions; this is shown above the Spawn Pool. If you click your Buttons right you'll get a Multitude of Minions.
Your minions will enter on the left and travel to the right attacking all Defenders within range. After attacking they will have to reload before attacking again.

Armory

You have access to the Armory!
Here you can upgrade your minions damage and health. All upgrades are applied when a minion is Deployed and does not effect already Deployed minions.

Armory tab has new equipment to increase damage and health; upgrades are small but stack multiplicatively.
Money Pit only increases the resources gained from Regroup, useful for dumping extra resources after upgrade limit has been reached.
Regroup will reset the Armory and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Gym

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Gym!
Here you can train your minions to increase move speed and rate of attack.
You can also unlock new minions.

Armory Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Armory.
Max Minions++ increases the maximum minions on deployed at once.
Unlock Automate Armory unlocks the ability to automatically purchase upgrades in the Armory. If all minion upgrades are maxed out you can also automatically Regroup.
Research will reset the Gym and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Tips:
A good place to start in the Gym is Unlock Automate Armory; this will unlock some checkboxes in the Armory that will automatically click buttons for you. A good goal is to have at least one Armory Effectiveness before your fifth Regroup. Armorty Effectiveness increases how the effect of Armory Upgrades. Initially each upgrade counts as one, for each Armory Effectiveness you get an additional Armory Upgrade for free with each Armory Upgrade purchased.

Lab

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Lab!
Here you can genetically motify your Minions to increase the Attack Range and Impact Radius.

You can also unlock Elementals to fight for you; each of the four Elemental types have unique abilities. See the Unit Info tab for more information.

Gym Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Gym.
Upgrade Limit++ increases the upgrade limit.
Unlock Automate Gym unlocks the ability to automatically purchase upgrades in the Gym. If all minion upgrades are maxed out you can also automatically Research.
Recruit will reset the Lab and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Tips:
If the spawn pools are stopped it means the Baracks is full; you could try unchecking some of the weaker minions. Try different combinations of active spawn pools.
Upgrade Limit++ will increase the maximum resource gain each reset.

Office

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Office and Bosses!
The Office has Boss enhancements and upgrades Minions Per Deploy and Spawn Delay. This will allow you to deploy MULTITUDES OF MINIONS!.

Minions Per Deploy increases the number of minions that will appear when deployed from the Baracks. Spawn Delay reduces the amount of time it takes to spawn a minion to the Baracks.

Lab Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Lab.
Deploy Time-- reduces the amount of time it takes to Deploy minions.
Unlock Automate Lab unlocks the ability to automatically purchase upgrades in the Lab. If all minion upgrades are maxed out you can also automatically Recruit.
Restructure will reset the Office and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Tips:
Different Bosses work better with different Minions. A couple good combinations include Pestilence+Fire and Death+Water.

Forge

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Forge!
You will now collect items from Heroes. You can equip items on your Boss.
You can select to automatically sell items below a certain item score.
Starting Level selects the level you will start at after a Regroup, Research, Recruit, or Restructure.

You can also Buy Back the most recently sold Item. If there is an Item available to Buy Back it will be visible under the Auto-Sell area.

Items have a type of item, score as well as attributes. The score is indicated in the top right of each item.
A higher level Hero will have the potential to a drop higher level Item.
The first attribute is unique to the type of item and only effects the Boss. Each additional attribute can effect a variety of targets and have a variety of effects.
When an item has reached its maximum potential it can be Reforged.

Lock an item by clicking on the button with a Lock icon. This will prevent accidentally selling it.
Sell as item to gain resources used to upgrade items
Equip an item to gain its bonuses.

Reroll will replace the attribute. This will reset any upgrades on that attribute.
Upgrade will increase the power of the attribute.
Prestige will increase the maximum power potential of the attribute
Reforge an item to increase the maximum potential.

Office Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Office.
Auto Sell Limit++ increases the maximum auto sell score. Items that are Locked or Equipped will not be auto-sold regardless of the score.
Starting Level++ increases the maximum starting level.

Tips:
Selling Items will get you more Vincula than Restructuring.

Store

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Store!
You can buy items, exchange resources, and buy transient blessings and curses.
You gain resources used in the store from maximizing item attributes.

Item Chest gives one random item with the score determined by various Feats and Chest Level.
Transient Boost either bless your Invaders or curse the Defenders.
Exchange 1 Womba for an amount of lower resources determined by Feats.

This is currently the last tab in the game and I don't have any plans for additional content.

Tips:
Get some items that have Vincula Discounts and upgrade some low score items for some cheap Womba.

Statistics

Shows Data related to each Unit Type.

Deploy Count counts the number of each unit type that was deployed, summoned, built, etc.
Damage Done counts actual damage done. It takes into account any damage reduction and does not count damage done over the targets current health. Damage Taken conts incoming damage. It takes the incoming damage before any damage reduction and counts incoming damage over the units current health.

Note: Damage Taken and Damage Done currently does not take into account damage over time.

Note: The notation for showing damage is [number of projectiles]x[damage]...[attack charges for a projectile]. So 2x4...3 will shoot 2 projectiles, each doing 4 damange and hitting 3 targets (bouncing twice). If it only has one projectile it omits the first part and if it only has one charge (no bounces) it omits the last part.

Clear Data and Start Over deletes any save cookies and reloads a fresh game from the beginning.

Unit Info

The Unit Info Tab contains information about different unit types. Click on the name of a unit for more details.

  • Health When a unit's health drops to zero it dies.
  • Damage The amount of health that will be removed from a target when it is hit by a projectile from this unit.
  • Move Speed How fast a unit moves (Towers do not move).
  • Attack Rate The amount of time between attacks.
  • Attack Range How far a unit can attack.
  • Spawn Delay The amount of time between spawning a unit.
  • Projectile Speed How fast a projectile travels. Note: Beam attacks attack instantly.
  • Impact Radius The size of the impact; effects Balistic and Blast type attacks.
  • Target Count The number of targets a unit can attack each time it reloads.
  • Attack Charges The number of targets each projectile can hit. 1 will hit an initial target, 2 will hit an initial target and bounce to one additional target, ...
  • Chain Range The maximum distance an attack can bounce between subsequent targets if the Attack Charges is greater than 1.
  • Chain Damage Reduction The damage reduction for each subsequent target. Next Damage = Current Damage * Chain Damage Reduction. If a unit does 10 Damage with .9 Chain Damage Reduction and 3 Attack Charges the Initial target will take 10 damage, the next target will take 9 damage, and the final target will take 8.1 damage.
  • Aura Range The distance an Aura will effect units. Note: Underlings, Minions, and Towers do not have Auras.
  • Aura Power The strength of an Aura. Note: Underlings, Minions, and Towers do not have Auras.
  • Ability Duration How long a Boss's Active Ability will last.
  • Ability Cooldown How long it takes to recharge a Boss's Active Ability after the Ability Duration has expired.
  • Damage Reduction Reduces the incoming damage from projectiles. Towers in a Castle and Templars have Damage Reduction.
  • Initial Minions Not currently used
  • Minions Per Deploy The number of Minions that are deployed to The Path each time that Minion Type is deployed.

The Unit Info Tab does not contain details about the Small Colorless Underlings. Their details are:
Health 1
Move Speed 10
Spawn Delay 100

Keyboard Shortcuts

Square brackets indicate a shortcut-key. For example [=] next to Help in the menu on the left indicates pressing the = button will take you to this Help tab.

There are a few hotkeys that are not documented in that manner.

Spacebar pauses and resumes the game.

M will enable all Minion Spawns. ALT+M will disable all Minion Spawns.

The Display Details in the Options tab can be toggled by holding CTRL+ALT+SHIFT and pressing the keys in the 4x4 grid on the left of a QWERTY keyboard. For example CTRL+ALT+SHIFT+1 will draw the range of each minion and CTRL+ALT+SHIFT+D will indicate the current health of each tower.

CTRL+Z will automatically use the Active Skill whenever available.
CTRL+Left/Right Arrows will change the Boss Position.

Game Mechanics/Notes

Here are some miscelanious game mechanics that don't really fit in any of the other sections but could be helpful.

  • Clicking on the Map will add a Mite to the Baracks.
  • if any Invaders that fall behind are left behind.
  • Towers within a Castle have increased defense.
  • The only point that matters for hit detection and checking range is the very center.
  • Blue projectiles and impacts are from the Defenders and only effect the Invaders.
  • Red projectils and impacts are from the Invaders and only effect the Defenders.
  • Towers will slowly repair overtime based on Level.
  • The further along the Path the longer it takes for minions to Deploy.
  • Minions can be healed up to a maximum of 4x their starting health by Water Minions Healing effects.
  • Minions Per Deploy also effects how much each minion counts towards 'Active Minions'. For 2 Minions Per Deploy, each minion counts as 1/2; 3 Minions Per Deploy, each minion counds as 1/3.
The Help Tab has information about the game and what effects clicking different buttons have. Each tab also has a help button to show the relevant help information for that tab.

About

A few years ago I set out to find an Idle Tower-Offense game; I couldn't find one so I decided to make one. Between everything else going on I would brush off my trusty text editor and push some buttons. I wanted to make this without the help of any additional libraries; this entire project was written in a combination of notepad++ and Caret.

The current art is fairly basic because I'm not an artist; I don't know anything about chiaroscuro shading or . I am looking into making some basic 8-bit style art but my priority was on making a playable game.
I hope you like it, but if not take your button clicks elsewhere.

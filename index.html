General Game Information

Welcome to Path Land! The Towers have been holding back the poor Underlings. Trapped, unable to click buttons and unable to progress down the path. With your advanced ability to click buttons you can unlock Minions and finally break through.

Underlings are weak, continually spawn, and are not upgradable. They do not show up in the Minions tab and you get 1 Ruple on their death.
With your awesome button clicking skills you can expand beyond these basic Underlings to more powerful Minions!

This is an Idle/Incremental Tower-Offense game. As an Idle/Incremental game it will progress without much input and your Invaders will get incrementally stronger over time; some browsers may slow down the game if it isn't the active tab. As a Tower-Offense game your objective is to destroy as many defenders as possible.
There is currently not an end or win condition and the game objective is simply to make more powerful Invaders. Some game mechanics do have limits such as Move Speed to avoid game breaking.

Minions spawn from the Spawn Pool into the Baracks and are then Deployed to the battle field.
You can view your current Minions, Spawn Pool, and Baracks in the Minions tab by clicking the Minions button in the menu on the left.
The further away from your home the longer it takes for minions to Deploy.
Your minions will enter on the left and travel to the right destroying all Defenders within range. After attacking they will have to reload before attacking again.
Invaders can travel at different speeds; if any Invaders fall too far behind are abandoned. Different types of minions are better suited for different situations. The Info tab has information on each type of unit. Click the unit name for additional details.

Each Castle is the home of at least one Hero that needs vanquishing, when all the vanquishing is done the Castle collapses. Towers and Heroes get stronger as you progress
Towers within a Castle have increased defense.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Square brackets indicate a shortcut-key. For example [=] next to Help in the menu on the left indicates pressing the = button will take you to this Help tab.
One area where the hotkeys are not documented in the interface is the Display Details in the Options tab. These checkboxes can be toggled by holding CTRL+ALT+SHIFT and pressing the keys in the 4x4 grid on the left of a QWERTY keyboard. For example CTRL+ALT+SHIFT+1 will draw the range of each minion and CTRL+ALT+SHIFT+D will indicate the current health of each tower.

Also important note: Space bar pauses.

Tips

Upgrade your minions as much as possible before Regrouping to maximize your ß gain. For your first Regroup aim for a bare minimum of 8ß but over 20ß is best.
Clicking on the Map will add a Mite to the Baracks.
The only point that matters for hit detection and checking range is the very center.
Unlock Automate Armory to automate clicking the Armory Buttons (unless you like manually clicking Buttons).
A good goal for your third or fourth Regroup is to be able to afford Armory Effectiveness; this will drastically increase your resource gain. Blue projectiles and impacts are from the Defenders and only effect the Invaders. Red projectils and impacts are from the Invaders and only effect the Defenders.
The notation for showing damage is [number of projectiles]x[damage]...[number of impacts for a projectile]; so 2x4...3 will shoot two projectiles, each doing 4 damange and hitting 3 targets (bouncing twice). If it only has one projectile it omits that portion and if it only has one target (no bounces) it omits that portion.
Try different combinations of active spawn pools. If the spawn pools are stopped it means the Baracks is full; you could try unchecking some of the weaker minions.
Upgrade Limit++ will increase the resource gain each reset.
Minions can be healed up to a maximum of 4x their starting health.
Minions Per Deploy also effects how much each minion counts towards 'Active Minions'. For 2 Minions Per Deploy, each minion counts as 1/2; 3 Minions Per Deploy, each minion counds as 1/3.
Different Bosses work better with different Minions. A couple good combinations include Pestilence+Fire and Death+Water.
CTRL+Z will automatically use the Active Skill whenever available.
CTRL+Left/Right Arrows will change the Boss Position.
Selling Items will get you more Vincula than Restructuring. Towers will slowly repair overtime.
Upgrade some low score items for some cheap Womba.

Armory

You have access to the Armory!
Here you can upgrade your minions damage and health. All upgrades are applied when a minion is Deployed and does not effect already Deployed minions.

Armory tab has new equipment to increase damage and health; upgrades are small but stack multiplicatively.
Money Pit only increases the resources gained from Regroup, useful for dumping extra resources after upgrade limit has been reached.
Regroup will reset the Armory and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Check back here after unlocking additional content for more information.

Gym

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Gym!
Here you can train your minions to increase move speed and rate of attack.
You can also unlock new minions.

Armory Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Armory.
Max Minions++ increases the maximum minions on deployed at once.
Unlock Automate Armory unlocks the ability to automatically purchase upgrades in the Armory. If all minion upgrades are maxed out you can also automatically Regroup.
Research will reset the Gym and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Feats

Feats track your progress and give passive bonuses to various aspects of the game.
Each Feat has a Name, Rank, Goal, and Perk.
The Name indicates what each feat is tracking.
The Rank consists of two parts; the Level and Maxout Count. The Level counts each time the Goal has been reached; each Feat has a Maximum level. When a Feat reaches the Maximum Level the Level resets to 0 and the Maxout Count increments by one.
The Goal shows your current amount and the target value to increase the Perk.
The Perk describes a benefit that is gained based on the current Rank.

More Feats will be unlocked as you unlock additional content.

The Feats tab also has your Overall Score, this score determines your offline resource gain.

Lab

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Lab!
Here you can genetically motify your Minions to increase the Attack Range and Splash Radius (Impact Size).

You can also unlock the four elements to fight for you; each of the four elements has unique abilities. See the Info tab for more information.

Gym Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Gym.
Upgrade Limit++ increases the upgrade limit.
Unlock Automate Gym unlocks the ability to automatically purchase upgrades in the Gym. If all minion upgrades are maxed out you can also automatically Research.
Recruit will reset the Lab and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Office

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Office and Bosses!
The Office has Boss enhancements and upgrades Minions Per Deploy and Spawn Delay. This will allow you to deploy MULTITUDES OF MINIONS!.

Minions Per Deploy increases the number of minions that will appear when deployed from the Baracks. Spawn Delay reduces the amount of time it takes to spawn a minion to the Baracks.

Lab Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Lab.
Deploy Time-- reduces the amount of time it takes to Deploy minions.
Unlock Automate Lab unlocks the ability to automatically purchase upgrades in the Lab. If all minion upgrades are maxed out you can also automatically Recruit.
Restructure will reset the Office and gain additional resources for more upgrades.

Boss

Boss Position determines how far the Boss should move.
Active Ability button will activate your current bosses active.

Forge

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Forge!
You will now collect items from Heroes. You can equip items on your Boss.
You can select to automatically sell items below a certain item score.
Starting Level selects the level you will start at after a Regroup, Research, Recruit, or Restructure.

Items have a type of item, score as well as attributes. The score is indicated in the top right of each item.
A higher level Hero will have the potential to a drop higher level Item.
The first attribute is unique to the type of item and only effects the Boss. Each additional attribute can effect a variety of targets and have a variety of effects.
When an item has reached its maximum potential it can be Reforged.

Lock an item by clicking on the button with a Lock icon. This will prevent accidentally selling it.
Sell as item to gain resources used to upgrade items
Equip an item to gain its bonuses.

Reroll will replace the attribute. This will reset any upgrades on that attribute.
Upgrade will increase the power of the attribute.
Prestige will increase the maximum power potential of the attribute
Reforge an item to increase the maximum potential.

Office Effectiveness increases the effect of each upgrade purchased in the Office.
Auto Sell Limit++ increases the maximum auto sell score. Items that are Locked or Equipped will not be auto-sold regardless of the score.
Starting Level++ increases the maximum starting level.

Store

Good button clicking! You have unlocked the Store!
You can buy items, exchange resources, and buy transient blessings and curses.
You gain resources used in the store from maximizing item attributes.

Item Chest gives one random item with the score determined by various Feats and Chest Level.
Transient Boost either bless your Invaders or curse the Defenders.
Exchange 1 Womba for an amount of lower resources determined by Feats.

This is currently the last tab in the game.

About

I made this game in my spare time clicking buttons and typeing in notepad++ and Caret. I made it without any libraries to see what making something like this would be like in pure JS. The current art is fairly basic because I'm not an artist; I am looking into making some basic 8-bit style art but my priority was on making a playable game.
